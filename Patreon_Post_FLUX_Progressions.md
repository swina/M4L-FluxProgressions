# 🚀 Introducing FLUX Progressions! (Formerly Expresso Chords)

Hey everyone! 👋

I'm super excited to share a major update with you all. As some of you know, I've been developing a Max for Live MIDI effect previously known as **Expresso**. Today, I’m thrilled to announce its a new device: **FLUX Progressions**! 🎹✨

### What is FLUX Progressions?
"FLUX Progressions" better captures the dynamic, evolving, and flowing nature of what this device actually does. It’s not just about playing static chords—it’s about giving them life, rhythm, and continuous motion.

### What makes FLUX Progressions special?
Here’s a look at some of the powerful features packed into this device:
* 🌀 **FLUX Sequencer:** Build complex, evolving chord progressions directly in Ableton Live. You can even input your own custom chord progressions directly from your MIDI keyboard!
* 🧠 **FLUX Generator:** A brand new progression generator that intelligently creates chords based on your chosen root note, musical style, and Major/Minor scale.
* 🎸 **FLUX Articulation Engine:** Add instant groove. Define custom rhythmic offsets (calculated perfectly as 16th notes) and use the "Cycle" option to repeat articulation patterns over sustained chords.
* 🎛️ **Latch & Pipe Modes:** Intelligent note routing that allows you to latch specific ranges (like deep bass notes) while piping melodies right through the sequencer.
* ❄️ **Sequencer Freeze:** A brand new feature that lets you instantly freeze the sequencer to infinitely sustain the current chord—perfect for building massive tension in your tracks!

This rebrand comes alongside a ton of under-the-hood stability improvements, bug fixes, and feature additions that were built directly from your feedback.
